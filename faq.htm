FAQ

Your questions, answered

Where does my donation actually go?
84 cents of every dollar goes directly to conservation programs. The remaining 16 cents covers administration, communications, and fundraising costs. Full audited financials are published annually on our Accountability page.
How is Verdant Horizon different from other environmental organisations?
We combine rigorous independent science, long-term field presence (often decades in the same landscape), and a commitment to community-led approaches. We also publish our failures — not just our successes — because we think the sector learns faster that way.
Can I volunteer without any experience?
Absolutely. Most of our volunteer roles don't require prior experience — just willingness to show up and follow guidance. We provide all training. Some specialist roles (like marine survey diving) have prerequisites, but these are clearly stated.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
In most countries, yes. We are a registered charity in the UK, EU, USA, Canada, and Australia. Check our Donate page for country-specific guidance, or contact us directly.
How do you measure conservation impact?
We use independently verified impact indicators for each program — species population trends, hectares of habitat protected, carbon sequestration rates, community wellbeing metrics, and more. All results are published in our annual Impact Report, including programs that underperformed.
Do you work with businesses and corporations?
Yes, selectively. We accept corporate partnerships only with organisations that have made credible, verified sustainability commitments. All partnerships are fully disclosed in our annual report. Any partner found misrepresenting their environmental record has their partnership immediately terminated.
Can I cancel my monthly donation at any time?
Yes, always. There are no minimum commitment periods and no cancellation fees. You can manage or cancel your giving directly through your account dashboard, or contact our supporter care team.

Still have questions?

Our supporter care team is here to help. We aim to reply to every enquiry within one working day.
