Leadership

Meet our leadership team

These are the people steering Verdant Horizon — scientists, advocates, and organisers who've dedicated their careers to protecting the natural world.

Dr. Elena Vasquez
Chief Executive Officer

Marine ecologist with 22 years in Pacific conservation. Former UNEP advisor. Speaks four languages, terrible at small talk.

Dr. Amara Osei
Chief Science Officer

Leads our global research network. PhD in ecosystem ecology from Accra. Has published 94 peer-reviewed papers and counting.

Priya Sharma
Director of Field Operations

Coordinates 1,200 field staff across 130 countries. Previously spent a decade with field teams in the Himalayas and Amazon basin.

Marcus Chen
Chief Policy Officer

Helped draft three major international climate accords. Believes policy change and grassroots action work best when they push together.

Lena Hartmann
Chief Financial Officer

Keeps the organisation lean and accountable. Former partner at a sustainability-focused investment firm. Obsessively transparent.

James Okafor
Director of Partnerships

Builds the relationships that make our work possible — with governments, Indigenous communities, and the private sector alike.

Sofia Reyes
Director of Communications

Believes science that nobody hears about doesn't save anything. Dedicated to making complex environmental issues genuinely understandable.

Dr. Tariq Rahman
Head of Climate Programs

Climate scientist and policy specialist. Leads our work on carbon markets, renewable transitions, and community resilience programs.
