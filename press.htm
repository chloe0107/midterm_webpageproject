Press & Media

Journalists, we're here to help. Press releases, expert contacts, image library, and background briefings — all in one place.

Home/Resources/Press & Media
BlogNewsFAQNewsletterPerspectivesReportsData & ToolsImpact ReportAnnual ReportPress & Media

We work with journalists across all media and we try to be as helpful as possible. If you're working on a story that touches on our work, please reach out — we'll do our best to respond quickly and give you something genuinely useful.

Press contact

For all media enquiries, contact our communications team at press@verdanthorizon.org. We aim to respond within four hours during business days. For urgent requests outside business hours, call our press line on +41 22 000 1235.

Expert spokespeople

Our team is available for comment, interview, and background briefing on a wide range of conservation topics. Dr. Elena Vasquez (marine ecosystems, conservation strategy), Dr. Amara Osei (tropical forests, biodiversity), Dr. Tariq Rahman (climate policy, carbon markets), and Marcus Chen (environmental policy, international agreements) are all available to media.

"Verdant Horizon spokespeople are always well-briefed, direct, and don't spin. That's rarer than it should be."

— Environmental correspondent, The Guardian
