2025 Annual Report
& Financial Statements

Full audited accounts, governance disclosures, and a plain-English breakdown of where every dollar went in 2025.

Every year since 1982 we've published our complete financial statements within 60 days of our year end. This is the 2025 report, covering the financial year January to December 2025.

Where the money came from

Total income in 2025 was $142.4 million. Individual donations accounted for 58% of income, institutional grants 28%, corporate partnerships 9%, and investment returns 5%. Our one million individual supporters remain the backbone of everything we do — it's that broad base of small donations that gives us the financial independence to take positions that institutions or corporations might not always like.

Where the money went

84 cents of every dollar went directly to conservation programs. Of the remaining 16 cents, 8 cents covered general administration, 5 cents went to fundraising costs, and 3 cents to communications and advocacy. We consider these ratios healthy for an organisation of our scale and complexity.

"These are among the cleanest financials I've seen in 20 years of auditing charitable organisations. The level of disclosure is exceptional."

— External Auditor, Grant Thornton (2025)

Governance disclosures

Our Board of Trustees held six meetings in 2025. Board member attendance, declarations of interest, and remuneration are all disclosed in full in section four of the report. No Board member received any personal benefit from the organisation beyond reasonable expenses.
