Get Involved

Put your hands
to work for nature

Whether you're a scientist, a storyteller, or someone who just wants to show up and help — there's a place for you in our volunteer community.

Home/Get Involved/Volunteer
Volunteer Attend Events Take Action Carbon Calculator Explore Places Educational Resources

Our 40,000-strong volunteer network is one of our most powerful assets. Volunteers run community programs, support field research, translate our science for local audiences, and do the kind of work that paid staff simply can't be everywhere to do.

You don't need a science degree or prior experience. You need to care, and you need to be willing to show up. We'll handle the training, the coordination, and the support.

"I started as a beach clean-up volunteer. Three years later I was coordinating marine monitoring surveys. Verdant Horizon gave me the skills and the confidence to do work I never imagined I could."

— Lena K., volunteer since 2021, Philippines

How volunteering works

Choose the type of volunteering that fits your life — a few hours on a weekend, a week-long field placement, or an ongoing remote role supporting our communications or data teams. We match volunteers with opportunities based on skills, location, and availability.

Habitat Restoration

Join planting days, invasive species removal events, and habitat restoration work in your local area or at one of our international field sites.

Sign up

Marine Survey Support

Certified divers and snorkellers can join our reef monitoring programs. No research experience needed — we provide full training and equipment.

Sign up

Community Education

Help us run environmental education workshops in schools, community centres, and local events. Especially valuable if you speak local languages.

Sign up

Remote & Digital Support

Writers, designers, translators, data analysts, and social media volunteers can contribute entirely remotely on a schedule that works for them.

Sign up

Farm & Food Programs

Support our regenerative farming partners — helping on demonstration plots, assisting with farmer training days, and documenting best practices.

Sign up

Events & Fundraising

Help us run events — from local clean-ups to major fundraising galas. Great for people who are organised, personable, and love getting things done.
