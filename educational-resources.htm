Free resources for students, teachers, and curious minds of all ages — because understanding the natural world is the first step to protecting it.

Primary School Pack

Age-appropriate lessons, activities, and videos on biodiversity, habitats, and what children can do to help. Aligned with national curricula in 12 countries. Free to download.

Secondary School Resources

In-depth modules on climate change, ecosystem services, conservation science, and policy. Includes data exercises using our real monitoring datasets.

University & Research

Open-access datasets, methodology guides, and case studies for undergraduate and postgraduate research. Our data portal gives access to 35 years of field monitoring data.

Online Learning Series

Six free self-paced courses covering the fundamentals of conservation biology, climate science, sustainable food systems, and environmental policy.
