Give

There's more than
one way to make a difference

Money is one thing. But your time, your skills, your network, and your voice are just as valuable to us.

Home/Give/Other Ways to Give

Workplace Giving

Many employers will match charitable donations pound for pound or dollar for dollar. Check with your HR team — it could double the impact of your gift at no extra cost to you.

Find out more

Run a Fundraiser

Birthday fundraisers, challenge events, community sales — whatever format works for you. We'll give you all the materials and support you need to make it a success.

Get started

Donate Your Skills

Are you a lawyer, accountant, designer, developer, or researcher? Pro bono professional services can be just as valuable as financial support, particularly for smaller regional programs.

Offer your skills

Spread the Word

Share our stories, follow us on social media, talk about our work with your friends and family. Growing our audience of engaged people is genuinely one of the most valuable things you can do.

Follow us

Corporate Partnership

If your company has a genuine commitment to sustainability and wants to do more than write a cheque, we're open to partnerships that involve real collaboration. We're selective, but we're listening.

Talk to us

Leave a Legacy Gift

A gift in your will is one of the most powerful things you can do for the natural world. It costs nothing today and can protect nature for generations after you're gone.

Learn more

Not sure which option is right for you?

Just reach out. We're happy to talk through what would work best for your situation — no pressure, no scripts.
