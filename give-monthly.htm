What a monthly gift adds up to

$10/month = $120/year = one full week of reef monitoring
$20/month = $240/year = one community forest guardian for six months
$50/month = $600/year = one hectare of high-carbon forest protected for a year
Why monthly giving matters

Consistent funding
changes everything

When we know what our income will be month to month, we can make multi-year commitments to field programs. We can hire local staff and keep them employed. We can sign long-term land protection agreements. One-off donations are wonderful — but monthly giving is what lets us plan.

Monthly donors also cost less to support than one-off donors, which means more of your money goes directly to the work. The average monthly donor gives 42% more per year than one-off donors because they stick with us through the quieter months as well as the big moments.

Rather give once?

That's completely fine too. Any support you can give makes a real difference.
