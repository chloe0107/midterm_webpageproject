Give

A gift that outlasts
all of us

Leaving a gift in your will is one of the most powerful things you can do for the natural world. It costs nothing today and it can protect nature for generations.

Legacy gifts — gifts made through your will or estate plan — are the single most significant source of funding for long-term conservation work. If you care about the natural world, this is one of the most meaningful things you can do.

What is a legacy gift?

A legacy gift (also called a bequest or planned gift) is a donation you arrange to make from your estate after you die. It can be a specific sum, a percentage of your estate, or a specific asset. It doesn't affect what you have available to you during your lifetime, and it can be changed at any time.

"I don't have a lot of spare cash right now. But I can make sure that the things I care about keep going after I'm gone. That feels right to me."

— Margaret H., legacy donor, age 61

How to leave a gift to Verdant Horizon

The simplest way is to include a clause in your will specifying a gift to Verdant Horizon. We can provide standard wording that your solicitor or attorney can use. You don't need to tell us, but many legacy donors do let us know — it helps us plan for the long term and means we can thank you personally.

What your gift could fund

Unless you specify otherwise, legacy gifts go into our core conservation endowment — funding the long-term field programs that are hardest to fund through annual giving. Some donors choose to direct their gift to a specific program or region. We're happy to discuss what matters most to you.

Questions we get asked

Do I need to tell you? No, but we'd love to know if you're comfortable sharing. Does it affect my will legally? No more than any other charitable bequest — your solicitor can advise. Can I change my mind? Yes, at any time. What's the minimum? There isn't one.

Talk to someone about this

Our planned giving team is here to answer questions, without any pressure. This is a personal decision and we want to help you feel confident about it.
