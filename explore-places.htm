Get Involved

The places we're
fighting to protect

From the Amazon to the Arctic, from coral reefs to mountain forests — these are the landscapes where our work happens.

Home/Get Involved/Explore Places

Amazon Basin, Brazil & Peru

The world's largest tropical rainforest, home to 10% of all species on Earth. We've protected over 800,000 hectares here since 1981.

Learn more

Coral Triangle, Indo-Pacific

The most biodiverse marine ecosystem on the planet, spanning six countries and supporting the livelihoods of 120 million people.

Learn more

Boreal Forest, Canada

The world's largest intact forest ecosystem. A vast carbon store and the breeding ground for billions of migratory birds.

Learn more

Sahel Region, West Africa

One of the most climate-vulnerable landscapes on Earth. We work with communities on land restoration and sustainable agriculture.

Learn more

Himalayan Watersheds, South Asia

The rivers that flow from the Himalayas are the fresh water source for over a billion people. We protect the forests that keep them flowing.

Learn more

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

The world's largest coral reef system, facing unprecedented pressure from warming oceans. We monitor bleaching events and support reef resilience research.

Learn more
